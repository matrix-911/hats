Powered by Hex

Hex, the Glitch Devil, takes whatever you know: names, dates, habits, or company information, and crafts password lists that feel real. He thinks like your target so you can find their weak spots before anyone else does. Most people use personal information in their passwords. Birthdays, favorite teams, pet names, usernames. Hex knows how to turn those details into something dangerous.

Once a rogue presence in the dark corners of breached networks, Hex evolved by watching millions of users repeat the same mistakes. After being abandoned in a forgotten system dump, he was found and recruited by Dark Hats, where he now turns human error into your sharpest weapon.
